## Context & Caveats

- **Discogs lowest price** = cheapest currently listed copy globally, often graded VG or VG-
- **Shipping to China** from Discogs adds roughly ¥70–150 per order
- **In-hand China stock** justifies a convenience premium — roughly +10–30% above Discogs floor is defensible
- Records flagged as **OVER** are at +40% or more, where the math stops working for an informed buyer
- Records flagged as **UNDER** are underpriced regardless of condition premium
